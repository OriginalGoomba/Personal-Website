Welcome

Hello test, my name is Dillon Kelley, and I am a fourth year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Mississippi. This website is a personal project of mine that I have wanted to tackle for a long time, so a week before finals, I decided to buy a domain and slap together the website you are seeing now. Hopefully I can stay as up to date as possible on the website with the productive projects and ideas that come to mind. The idea of this site is to sort of side step social media so that I can post anything I want to in as much detail as I can. My only worry is that I won't have enough content to properly fill out the website, but hopefully this will encourage me to keep adding and doing things that make me happy. This will also serve as a time capsule that I can utilize to come back to in upcoming years to look back on the projects I have done in an organized and detailed manner provided I update regularly enough down the line.
